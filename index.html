About Us

Pawan Dairy Products has been a trusted company serving people with the finest quality products for over 22 years.
Since the very first day, our purpose has been to serve the health of our customers for which dairy products have been the best for centuries in India. We have been a trustworthy supplier to a lot of customers including reputed restaurants, hotels, and caterers. Our U.S.P. has been the quality of products offered by us, be it malai paneer, desi ghee, khoya, curd, white butter, or soya chaap.
